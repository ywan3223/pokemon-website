  • Bulbasaur

    Pokemon GO Bulbasaur is a Grass and Poison type Pokemon with a max CP of 1115, 118 attack, 111 defense and 128 stamina in Pokemon GO. It was originally found in the Kanto region (Gen 1). Bulbasaur is vulnerable to Fire, Flying, Ice and Psychic type moves. Bulbasaur is boosted by Sunny and Cloudy weather. Bulbasaur's best moves are Tackle and Power Whip (7.96 DPS).
